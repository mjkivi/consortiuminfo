Right from the Start: Tips on Starting an Association or Consortium Off on the Right Foot
When: Friday, January 28th
Time Noon EST
Cost: Complimentary
Who Should
Attend:		 Board members, committee members, directors of standards of associations, consortia or other non-profit organizations

Starting an association or consortium is a complex endeavor, especially in the critical first few months. If you are starting an organization, get valuable advice on how to drive it forward. Join the experts as they share their real-world experiences and best practices so you can be on the road to success.

  • Learn the importance of carefully crafting the board
  • Explore the connection between budget and dues
  • Get advice on developing an effective sales plan
  • ...and much more.

This webcast features two experts who have launched and advised literally hundreds of associations, including some of the most influential in the financial, technology and other vital sectors. Join Andy Freed and Andy Updegrove as they share information and anecdotes gathered from their many years of real-world experience delivering effective guidance and leadership to associations and consortia.

Questions? Contact Steve O'Neill at soneill@virtualmgmt.com.

Andy Freed, who has more than 20 years of experience working with associations from small startups to multi-million dollar international organizations, is Executive Vice President and COO of Virtual, Inc., an award-winning association management company.
