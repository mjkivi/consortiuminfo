Open Buying on the Internet Consortium (OBI)

Overview: OBI is a global, multi-class consortium, which develops open standards for business-to-business Internet commerce. To accomplish that, it develops and promotes standards for Internet purchases: small dollar, non-production goods and services. Membership is open to all buying and selling organizations, technology providers, financial institutions and others. Its founding members were large buyers and sellers who wanted to make their Internet buying and selling simpler and more efficient.

Specifications: Identifies specific steps in the existing purchasing process, and provides standard communications elements so buyers and sellers have a common starting point. http://www.openbuy.org/specs/
